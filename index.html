Video Synthesis and 3D Vision

I am a 4th-year Ph.D. student at the University of Maryland College Park, advised by Jia-Bin Huang. My research interests include 3D computer vision and video synthesis, particularly focusing on reconstructing real-world scenes from casual videos for synthesis and editing.

My research collaborations include recent internship at Adobe (collaborating with Zhengqi Li, Eli Shechtman, Zhoutong Zhang, Joon-Young Lee, Gabriel Huang, and Jui-Hsien Wang) and prior experiences as a student researcher at Google DeepMind (mentored by Forrester Cole, Erika Lu, Tali Dekel, and Sarah Rumbley), and an earlier internship at Adobe (working with Feng Liu, Zhoutong Zhang, Kevin Blackburn-Matzen, Simon Niklaus, and Jianming Zhang).

[New] I am highly interested in an industrial full-time research position. Please feel free to connect.
