Yao-Chih Lee

CS Ph.D. Student at the University of Maryland
3D Vision and Video Synthesis

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the University of Maryland College Park, advised by Jia-Bin Huang. My research interests include 3D computer vision and video synthesis, particularly focusing on reconstructing real-world scenes from casual videos for synthesis and editing.

I had the privilege of working as a student researcher at Google DeepMind, collaborating with Forrester Cole, Erika Lu, Tali Dekel, and Sarah Rumbley. Previously, I was fortunate to work with Feng Liu, Kevin Blackburn-Matzen, Simon Niklaus, Jianming Zhang, and Zhoutong Zhang at Adobe Research; Chu-Song Chen at Academia Sinica; Yi-Ping Hung at National Taiwan University; and Kuan-Wen Chen at NCTU, Taiwan.
