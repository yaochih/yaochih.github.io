Yao-Chih Lee 李曜至

I am a first-year CS Ph.D. Student at University of Maryland, College Park advised by Prof. Jia-Bin Huang. My research interests lie in the fields of 3D Computer Vision, Computational Photography, and Machine Learning.

Prior to this, I am honored to work with Prof. Chu-Song Chen at Academia Sinica, Taiwan. I received my M.S from National Taiwan University advised by Prof. Yi-Ping Hung, and B.S. from National Chiao Tung University advised by Prof. Kuan-Wen Chen.
